FAQ

Why was this program created?
This program was created for students by students. Our product is for financial professionals and rookies. Our goal is to summarize key financial data from tedious and long K-10 forms
What type of information do you extract?
We extract the company overview, key risk factors, senior management staff and key financial data. Some of the key financial data we extract include sources of revenue, annualized income, liabilities and overall stock performance.
The report doesn't generate all the info that I want...
We are constantly improving our product. Please use the submission form below if you would like us to include specific features of the K-10 form in our future report versions.
Who are the creators of this platform?
This platform was created by four biomedical engineers from McMaster University: Benjamin Sun, Ishan Vermani, Anjola Adewale and Sheridan Fong
